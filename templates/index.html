NERC Outlines Steps for Resolving Electricity Complaints Nationwide

The Nigerian Electricity Regulatory Commission (NERC) has issued a step-by-step guide for consumers facing power supply and billing issues, urging them to follow established complaint channels for prompt resolution.

According to NERC, consumers should first contact their Distribution Company (DisCo) customer care unit for faults, outages, metering concerns, or billing disputes. Where a complaint remains unresolved, customers in states with a State Electricity Regulator (SER) should escalate to that authority. For all others, NERC's call centre is available:

📞 0201 344 4331  |  0908 899 9244
📧 consumeraffairs@nerc.gov.ng

OCCPA encourages all Ondo State residents to make use of these channels to assert their consumer rights.

Nov 2025

OCCPA Clamps Down on Unfair Trade Practices in Akure Filling Stations

The Ondo State Competitive and Consumer Protection Agency (OCCPA) has intensified enforcement actions against unfair trade practices in filling stations across Akure.

During routine inspections, the agency monitored business activities to ensure compliance with consumer protection regulations, including transparent pricing, product quality standards, and fair commercial practices.

OCCPA reaffirmed its mandate to safeguard consumer rights and promote a fair, competitive marketplace across the state.

Jan 2026

Safety of Consumers: NSCDC Partners Ondo Consumer Protection Agency

In a bid to ensure the safety and well-being of consumers in Ondo State, OCCPA has solicited the partnership of the NSCDC, Ondo State command.

This partnership is to protect and promote the rights of consumers — safeguarding their right to safety, competitive pricing, value for purchase, standardised goods & services, and access to a consumer redress platform.

The partnership was formalised during a familiarization visit to State Commandant CC Oluyemi Joshua by Administrative Secretary Hajia Kudirat Ola and her management team.
