Who We Are

The Ondo State Consumer Protection Agency is dedicated to protecting consumer rights and promoting fair, transparent market practices. We are committed to addressing consumer complaints, enforcing compliance, and strengthening public confidence in the marketplace. Our mission is to serve the people with integrity, professionalism, and efficiency. Together, we will ensure a safe and fair trading environment for all citizens.

Latest updates, programs, and enforcement activities across Ondo State

Nov 2025

Ondo State Competitive & Consumer Protection Agency clamps down on unfair trade practices in Akure filling stations

The Ondo State Competitive and Consumer Protection Agency (OCCPA) has intensified enforcement actions against unfair trade practices in filling stations across Akure.

During routine inspections, the agency monitored business activities to ensure compliance with consumer protection regulations, including transparent pricing, product quality standards, and fair commercial practices.

OCCPA reaffirmed its mandate to safeguard consumer rights and promote a fair, competitive marketplace across the state.

Jan 2026

SAFETY AND WELLBEING OF CONSUMERS, NSCDC PARTNERS ONDO CONSUMER PROTECTION AGENCY

In a bid to ensure the safety and well-being of consumers in Ondo State, the Competition and Consumer Protection Agency has solicited the partnership of the NSCDC, Ondo State command.

This partnership is to protect and promote the rights of consumers, safeguard consumers' right to safety, right to competitive price, value for purchase, standardised goods & services, provision of consumer redress platform, and many more. The partnership between both agencies was made at a familiarization visit made to the State Commandant, CC Oluyemi Joshua's Ibiloye cpo,mspsp,fwip by the Administrative Secretary, Mrs Hajia Kudirat Ola and her management staff.
