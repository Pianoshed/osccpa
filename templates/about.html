About the Agency

OCCPA safeguards consumer rights and promotes fair competition across Ondo State.

Who We Are

OCCPA is a statutory government institution established to protect consumers from unfair trade practices.

Our Mission

To safeguard consumers through effective regulation, enforcement, advocacy, and public sensitization, while promoting ethical trade practices and sustainable socio-economic development.

Core Responsibilities

  • Consumer rights protection
  • Market regulation
  • Price monitoring
  • Dispute resolution
  • Market surveillance

Our Vision

To establish a fair, safe, and transparent marketplace where consumer rights are fully protected and respected.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Professionalism
  • Fairness
  • Collaboration
