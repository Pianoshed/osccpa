About the Agency

OCCPA safeguards consumer rights and promotes fair competition across Ondo State, ensuring transparency, accountability, and justice in the marketplace.

Our Foundation

Understanding who we are and what drives our mission

Who We Are

OCCPA is a statutory government institution established to protect consumers from unfair trade practices, ensuring a safe and transparent marketplace for all citizens of Ondo State.

Our Mission

To safeguard consumers through effective regulation, enforcement, advocacy, and public sensitization, while promoting ethical trade practices and sustainable socio-economic development across Ondo State.

Core Responsibilities

  • Consumer rights protection
  • Market regulation & surveillance
  • Price monitoring & control
  • Dispute resolution services
  • Public education & awareness

Our Vision & Values

The principles that guide our work and commitment to excellence

Our Vision

To establish a fair, safe, and transparent marketplace where consumer rights are fully protected and respected, fostering trust and confidence in business transactions throughout Ondo State.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity in all operations
  • Transparency & accountability
  • Professionalism & excellence
  • Fairness & impartiality
  • Collaboration & partnership
